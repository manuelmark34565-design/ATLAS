# Product Specification

## Vision
ATLAS AI Employee is a no-code AI platform for small and medium businesses to deploy an AI assistant for customer support, lead capture, and business operations.

## Key Capabilities
- Train AI from business information and FAQs
- Provide 24/7 website chat support
- Capture qualified leads automatically
- Offer an owner dashboard for conversation and lead review

## Primary Users
- Business owners
- Sales/operations teams
- Customer support teams

## Core Pages
- Home
- Chat / AI assistant
- Pricing
- Sign In / Sign Up
- Privacy

## Non-Core / Future
- Advanced marketing pages
- Complex Stripe checkout integration
- Full multi-user organization management
